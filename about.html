About

What makes me a 'Digital Carpenter?' The ability to see things through to the end. Building with the materials and tools available to you. And most importantly having pride in the work you do.

Growing up helping my parents construct and renovate homes I learned that no matter how great the vision no project is ever perfect. The goal is to get the job done on time and under budget. Today as a web developer I draw many parallels between the work I do today and the work I grew up doing as a kid.

I was born and raised to build things. Today I do most of that building digitally. Let's build something great together.
